About Us

IncluModels is more than an agency; it's a revolutionary force redefining the modeling landscape. Founded by Selyush Chitikana, a former commercial model who witnessed firsthand the limitations placed on beauty and opportunity, IncloModels is fueled by a burning desire to rewrite the narrative.

Vision

IncluModels envisions a future where the modeling industry embraces diversity in all its glorious forms.

Mission

  • Champion Uncompromising Inclusivity
  • Empower Through Accessible Education
  • Redefine "Full-Time Model"

Goals

  • Establish IncluModels as a Global Leader for Inclusivity
  • Break Down Barriers with Affordability
  • Cultivate Inclopreneurs
